All Kinds of Experience!

Calm Adventure

The "Calm Adventure" is perfect for the people that just want a relaxing trip through nature's beauty, enjoy the view, hang out with friends or family, all while practicing an exciting sport in water!

Explorer Trip

The "Explorer Trip" is a camping trip designed for people that want to live the Raft Voyage experience while staying dry, we feel you, and we want to give you the beatiful sights of the mountains and the forests around!

We're Cooked Adventure

The "We're Cooked Adventure", as the name states, was prepared for the bravest rafters out there who want to experience the adrenaline of extreme rafting at full speed, literally. This adventure is guided by professionals and counts with all required security measures.
