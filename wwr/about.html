Raft Voyage

"I had the time of my life with Raft Voyage! I went to the Skull Trip with my friends and it was madness, we were going full speed while the instructor guided us towards the right path. It felt like an adventure through the rage of the river, but instead of being afraid, we had lots of fun".

History

In 1967, George Voyage got lost in the forest with his company of boyscouts, but being well prepared, they used they're knowledge to endure in the middle of nowhere until they could find help. They went up and down gathering resources and exploring the forest, looking for a way out.

Eventually, they decided to go down the river in an improvised raft until they could reach the nearest settlement. With great struggles, they managed to find help and were rescued. Years later, George came back to the forest to create an enterprise where people could have fun and experience th thrill of nature.
