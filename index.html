Buenos Aires

Buenos Aires is the main province of Argentina, located in the East of the country, it is bordered by Entre Ríos, Santa Fe, Córdoba, La Pampa and Río Negro, having access to the sea from the East. Ciudad Autónoma is the capital city of the province, as well as a major tourist destination; it is, as well, the main economic center of the country due to its port activity
